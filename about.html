ABOUT US

Our goal for this app is to have a collective dad-jokes on the whim. Say your stuck in an awkward confrontation, just pull out your phone, login, and you'll have instant access to hundreds of dad jokes to get your rear away from the horn of the steer. We are all students of Lambda School except our head honcho Don Whitely who is our gracious OverLord Tyrant....er...i mean Project Lead. We hope with this app, you'll find a simple laugh that can break the ice in any tense situations. Find out who we are by scrolling down.
